Powered by Purpose, Driven by Change

NE-HEALS is a community-driven non-profit organization working to improve healthcare, education, and sustainable livelihoods for marginalized communities across Northeast India.

🏥 Healthcare Access

Promoting preventive care, health awareness programs, and affordable healthcare services for communities.

📚 Education

Improving literacy and education through schools, libraries, digital learning, and community programs.

🌿 Cultural Preservation

Protecting indigenous knowledge, traditions, and languages through education and documentation.

💼 Livelihood & Skills

Empowering communities through vocational training, entrepreneurship, and sustainable livelihood initiatives.
